My major currently is Pre-Business, as I intend to focus on Marketing later down the line. However, I also plan on pursuing a minor in Computer Science, as many of my interests also cover that subject area. Business has always been very interesting to me, even as I was growing up. I always found various ways to make money, whether that was through a certain mechanic in a video game or through a personal project I created in real life.

Although it may seem as a burden at first, the experience and advice my family was able to provide throughout my undergraduate years greatly helped me grow as a person. This advice strengthened my mind in properly handling business affairs, and furthered my career as an entrepreneur.
